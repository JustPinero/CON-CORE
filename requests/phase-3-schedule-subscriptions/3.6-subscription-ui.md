## Objective

Build Subscriptions station UI: scrollable list of detected subscriptions with monthly cost, category, status. Total monthly spend and annual projection at top. Detail view for individual subscription with lifetime spend calculation.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Subscription list renders with service name, cost, category
- [ ] Total monthly spend displayed at top
- [ ] Annual projection (monthly x 12) displayed
- [ ] Selecting a subscription shows detail view
- [ ] Detail view shows: lifetime spend, first detected, last charge, category
- [ ] "DRAFT CANCELLATION EMAIL" button on detail view
- [ ] Uses CRT styling throughout

## Files to Touch

src/stations/subscriptions/SubscrStation.tsx, src/stations/subscriptions/SubscrList.tsx, src/stations/subscriptions/CostSummary.tsx

## Tests to Write

- Subscription list renders all subscriptions
- Total monthly spend calculates correctly
- Annual projection is monthly x 12
- Detail view shows correct subscription info
- CRT styling applied (uppercase labels, correct colors)
